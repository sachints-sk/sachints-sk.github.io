What IS Android Campus Fest

Android Campus Fests are Google's Android Educator / Partner-led events on Modern Android Development hosted in universities/institutes independently or as tracks part of existing university campus fests. Android Campus Fest can be organised in Online / Offline/ Hybrid modes featuring Googlers, Edu Partners or Industry Experts to create the discussion spaces for all things on Android from November to December 2022.
